## About TFC

TFC (Tasty Fried Chicken) is a premium fast-food brand specializing in crispy fried chicken, juicy burgers, and refreshing beverages. We pride ourselves on using high-quality ingredients, unique recipes, and providing exceptional customer service.

Our mission is to bring the tastiest fried chicken experience to every neighborhood, with a vision to expand through our franchise model across the region.
